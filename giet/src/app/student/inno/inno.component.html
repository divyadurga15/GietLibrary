About

Innovation Club has emerged with an initiative to give students a place to think creatively. Our practical exercises foster participation, creativity, and innovation. Students also incorporate expertise and information from a variety of disciplines, including science and engineering. Our goal is to encourage an innovative culture and an atmosphere that stimulates the development of new ideas. Mission - To produce innovative, entrepreneurial and successful engineers and technologists of high calibre for the nation, to serve as a valuable resource for industry and society.

Objective

-->Through a variety of activities, the goals are to foster interests, creativity, innovation, and ethics. -->To support young students as they develop new ideas into prototypes in order to inspire, motivate, and nurture them. -->to inspire and prepare students for the pursuit of own interests, artistic endeavours, novel concepts, etc.

Activities

1.Education on startups and innovation 2.The Development of Startup Business Ideas for Innovation and Entrepreneurship 3.Presenting a technological poster 4.TECH TALK 5.IDEATHON 5 6.Regular workshops on issue solving, coming up with fresh ideas, and finding answers 7.Frequent documentaries on innovators and inventions
